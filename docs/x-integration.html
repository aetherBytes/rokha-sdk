03

How to connect — step by step

Five steps from a fresh login to your first run on your own X account.

1

Log in to rokha.ai

Open rokha.ai and sign in with Google or a Web3 wallet. That's the only account you need — Rokha holds every connection under it.

You land on your home, signed in.
2

Connect your X account

Go to your profile and press Link X. Approve Read and write when X asks — write access is what lets a tool post for you. This stamps your page and stores the connection your rigs use.

Profile›API Keys›Connected services›Connect X
Your @handle + 𝕏 Linked badge appear on your page. Set it active in Profile › Page.
3

Add an X tool

From the Registry — or the builder page at rokha.ai/@sage — adopt a Gust rig, or target X · Official MCP to reach all 119 operations. Search for:

searchgust
The rig lands on your canvas, its steps ready.
4

Attach your connection

On the step, add a Saved key so the rig runs on your account. Use the alias and header hint below — the token resolves to your own connected X, never the creator's.

aliasoauth-x
hintheader:Authorization:Bearer
The step shows a resolved connect-key, locked to x.com.
5

Name it and run

Give the rig a name, type your search query or tweet text as the input, and hit Run. Rokha makes the real call to X and shows you the trace — a post returns a real tweet id; a search returns real results.

A green trace with your posted tweet id — live, not simulated.
